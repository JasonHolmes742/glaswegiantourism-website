The city of glasgow or "glesga" in scots and "glaschu" in scottish gaelic is located on the banks of the River Clyde, in West Central Scotland. It is the biggest city in Scotland by both area and population. Renowned for its striking architecture most famously victorian and gothic. It is home to some of the UK's best music venues and events. Glasgow is also known for its shopping scene, art galleries, fascinating museums, botanic gardens, universities, cathedrals, restaurants and art scenes like the glasgow murals.
